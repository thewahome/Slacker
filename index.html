Welcome to Slacker.

Slacker is a Windows WPF application to help Slack user get notification when their team have new message.

How to Installation

You can download Slacker installation from Here. This is a OnceClick installation package. After downloaded, just one click on your Windows system. It will help you to install Slacker.

How to Use

When you open Slacker, you can see it on your taskbar. Move your mouse and click it on Slacker icon. Click "Setting". You can add your team to Slacker.

Go to Slack API and scroll page to "Authentication" section. You can see your team and token here. Copy and paste into Setting Window of Slacker, then save it. Slacker will check every 3 minutes ( you can can the interval from Setting Window ) and popup when you have new message from your team.
